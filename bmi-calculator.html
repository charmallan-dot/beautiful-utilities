📊 BMI Categories

Category BMI Range Health Risk
● Underweight < 18.5 Malnutrition risk
● Normal Weight 18.5 - 24.9 Low risk
● Overweight 25 - 29.9 Moderate risk
● Obese (Class I) 30 - 34.9 High risk
● Obese (Class II) 35 - 39.9 Very high risk
● Obese (Class III) ≥ 40 Extremely high risk

⚠️ Important Disclaimer

BMI is a useful screening tool but has limitations. It doesn't directly measure body fat or account for:

  • Muscle mass (athletes may have high BMI but low body fat)
  • Age (elderly may have more body fat at same BMI)
  • Sex (women typically have more body fat than men)
  • Bone density and frame size
  • Ethnicity (different populations have different risk thresholds)

Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized health advice. BMI should be used alongside other assessments.
