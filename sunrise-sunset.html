📸 Perfect for Photographers

Golden hour occurs shortly after sunrise and before sunset, providing the best natural light for photography. Blue hour happens just before sunrise and after sunset, creating stunning atmospheric conditions.

💡 Did You Know?

  • The earliest sunset doesn't occur on the winter solstice due to the equation of time
  • Northern locations have longer summer days and shorter winter days
  • At the poles, the sun can stay above or below the horizon for months
  • Solar noon is when the sun reaches its highest point (not always 12:00 PM)
  • Day length changes most rapidly around the equinoxes
