🌙 Fun Moon Facts

  • The lunar cycle is approximately 29.53 days long
  • A "Blue Moon" is the second full moon in a calendar month
  • The moon affects ocean tides through gravitational pull
  • Full moons have inspired countless myths and legends across cultures
  • The same side of the moon always faces Earth (tidal locking)
